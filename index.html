CHRISTMAS

Magical Christmas is the best time for charity, because it is believed that the good that a person does will definitely return to him. Therefore, we would like to invite you to join us and make Christmas special for children who have no family.

Christmas is celebrated to remember the birth of Jesus Christ. Christians believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God. The name Christmas comes from the mass of Christ.

About us

Christmas is not only an opportunity to gather with the whole family, but also to do something good and useful for those who are less fortunate. We want to help children who are left without a family. Namely, to make their Christmas a real fairy tale. But for this, each of us needs to unite and make a small effort, because Christmas is associated with the word "together".

What can we do?

  • Donate

    You can contribute in the form of money. And we will buy gifts and decorate a wonderful holiday!

  • Gifts

    Or you can give the children gifts made by yourself.It is not for nothing that they say that such a gift is special, because it is made with warmth, care and attention!

  • Santa

    You can experience the life of a real Santa and spend an unforgettable time with the children.You will give gifts, play, dance and most importantly have fu!n
